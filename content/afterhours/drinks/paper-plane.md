---
title: Paper Plane
---

## Ingredients

* 3/4 ounce bourbon
* 3/4 ounce Aperol
* 3/4 ounce Amaro Nonino Quintessentia
* 3/4 ounce lemon juice

## Instructions

1. Add all ingredients into a shaker
1. Add ice and shake until well-chilled
1. Strain into a coupe glass
