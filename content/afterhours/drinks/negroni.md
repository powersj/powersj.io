---
title: Negroni
---

## Ingredients

* 1 ounce gin
* 1 ounce Campari
* 1 ounce sweet vermouth

## Instructions

1. Add all ingredients to a mixing glass
1. Add ice and stir until well-chilled
1. Strain into a rocks glass over a large ice cube
1. Garnish with an orange peel
