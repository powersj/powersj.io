---
title: Whiskey Sour
---

## Ingredients

* 1.5 ounces bourbon
* .75 ounce lemon juice
* .75 ounce simple syrup

## Instructions

1. Add all ingredients into a shaker
1. Add ice and shake
1. Strain into a glass with ice
